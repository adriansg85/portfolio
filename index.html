RoadTrip MX

This project is intended on helping people determine the full cost of a road trip and split the price with their friends. First you need to choose the event you wish to attend. Then, to use the tool you first need to select the city for the event. For now, only the 3 main cities are avaliable (Mexico City, Monterrey, Guadalajara), Then select your date, starting point and the number of people going on the road trip. After this you can choose the events listed below the map and click in the hotel you wish to stay. When you finish you should see the full price displayed, this price includes tolls, fuel, hotel and tickets.

Deliver App

Deliver app is a a registry of deliveries or in-person meetings between two people. Any user can act as the deliverer or receiver and push the details of the meeting to an online database with this simple app.

The app allows any user to self identify as a deliverer or a receiver for each interaction. Receivers are given a QR code that deliverers can scan with their phones. This prompts the deliverer to request a signature from the receiver. At the end of the interaction, the app pushes the details of the meeting to a mySQL database.

Assets Overlord

Assets Overlord is an automated inventory system using RFID infrastructure. This type of system provides a 98% accuracy on inventory, as opposed to 65% when using bar codes. In addition to that, this also saves a lot of days of man-hours of inventory inspection. Knowing in real time the product availability, helps boost sales (around an 8% bump). And last but not least, theft prevention is also a very important factor in low-trust environments like Mexico.
